DONORS FAQ

1).WHO ARE ELGIBLE TO BECOME A DONOR IN INDO BLOOD?
Ans) The primary elgibilty rule for becoming a donor in the indo blood is that the person must be above the age of 18.
The donor must be without any long term or short term diseases.

The donor must be not habitad to the intake of any type of alkaloids or his blood shouldnt contain any alkaloid content

2) CAN MY NAME BE REMOVED IF I OPT TO COME OUT OF THE DONOR PROGRAMME?
Ans) Yes, ofcourse. If you wish your name to be removed from the donor programme, just write a mail to removemyname.indoblood@gmail.com. Then your name will be removed from the donor programme and you will get a deletion email to your account

3)IF I OPT TO COME OUT OF DONOR PROGRAMME, CAN I RE-ENTER IT?
Ans) Yes , of course. You can re enter into the programme by regestering newly to the donor account

4)IF I ENTERED THE PROGRRAME HOW LONG MY NAME WILL BE STORED IN THE SERVERS?
Ans) Your name will stay till 2-4 years in our servers. If your name is going to be removed we will send you a mail to register newly into our donor programme

5)WHY WERE THE DONOR NAMES REMOVED FROM THE SERVERS AFTER THE SPAN OF 2-4 YEARS?
Ans) To increase the accuracy of the site we will remove the donors name for 2-4 years (or) with their resubmission we will continue their name for the next term
We will do this thing to delete the inactive donors from the servers from the list and make it easy for a reciever to find a donor in short time.

6) CAN I COLLECT MONEY FROM THE RECIEVERS AS I AM GIVING THEM BLOOD?
Ans) If you do so your account will be permenantly blocked by our site and you cant enter it any more at any cost.
As if it was the site where the recieivers will get donors{(Who will donate)(Donate=Giving in sake of public service)}.
If the recievers will give you (at their sake) then you can take it as a gift. But if you demand then the actions taken will be tottaly strict.

7)HOW MANY TIMES WILL I GET A CALL FOR DONOTING BLOOD?
Ans) It may be not sure but we will check that you will get a chance of donating blood only 3-4 times per year(As recomended by WHO)

8) I HAD DONATED BLOOD IN BLOOD BANKS, THEN HOW CAN I MAKE IT TO BE INFORMED TO YOU?
Ans) To inform that information to us we request you to write a mail to informaboutme.indoblood@gmail.com

9) FOR DONATING BLOOD WHERE I SHOULD GO?
Ans) You will get the blood donation requests from the area which you had entered in the registration form. The zone of 50-100km from the entered area requests may come to you according to the availability

10) WHY IS PASSWORD REQUIRED IN THE REGISTRATION FORM?
Ans) There was a valid reason for it. For updating your account info you must enter even the password in the mail to confirm that was you.
